At-Home Care Essentials

Once a week, I detox my scalp with a clarifying shampoo. It’s amazing for removing buildup and resetting my hair. After that, I use the K18 Molecular Repair Mask. I apply 1-3 pumps, wait for 4 minutes, and let it work its magic, repairing and strengthening my strands. Finally, I finish with the Christophe Robin Regenerating Mask for hydration and softness—it’s like a spa day for my hair!

Winter Hair Color Trends

This winter, I’m loving cozy tones that feel both warm and elegant. Soft neutral golds add just the right amount of radiance, velvety chocolate browns bring a polished richness, and ruby reds strike the perfect balance between bold and natural. Pair these shades with a nutrient-rich gloss to keep your hair shiny and vibrant all season long.

Tools I’m Obsessed With

Having the right tools makes a huge difference in my routine. I use the Tangle Teezer Ultimate Detangler after washing my hair—it’s gentle and doesn’t pull or break strands. For tying up my hair, I’ve switched to soft scrunchies to avoid breakage. Sleeping on a silk pillowcase has been a game-changer for reducing frizz, and my shower filter helps keep my hair shiny by removing impurities from the water.
