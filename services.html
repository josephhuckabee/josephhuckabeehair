Services

Consultations

New clients and existing clients who'd like to try something new, please book a consultation.

Color

  • Brow Color & Bleaching
  • Gloss – Adds shine and enhances tone; perfect for refreshing faded color or adding richness.
  • Base Break – Softens natural root color for a brighter, more blended grow-out.
  • Single Process – One all-over color applied to roots only — grey coverage, going darker, or two levels lighter than natural base.

Bleach

  • Face Frame Highlight – Lightens the hair around your face to brighten and contour.
  • Partial Highlight – Highlights focused on the top and sides for a natural, sun-kissed effect.
  • Full Highlight – Highlights placed throughout the entire head for maximum dimension.
  • Bleach and Tone (Roots Only) – Lightens and tones regrowth for a consistent, solid color.
  • Bleach and Tone (Whole Head) – A full-head lightening and toning service for a major color shift.

Styling & Treatments

  • Blowout with Deep Conditioning Mask
  • Olaplex or K18 Treatment
  • Lasio Keratin Smoothing Treatment – Smooths and reduces frizz for shinier, more manageable hair and quicker styling.
