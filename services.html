Color menu

Considered color, finished with restraint.

A focused menu for tone, blonding, and editorial color work. Each service is chosen around condition, placement, and the kind of grow out that still feels intentional weeks later.

Tone

Gloss

30 minutes

A soft reset for shine, richness, and tonal balance. Ideal when the color is still working, but needs polish.

Blend

Base Break

30 minutes

A subtle shift at the root to soften contrast, brighten the natural base, and make blonding feel more seamless.

Depth

Single Process

1.5 hours

Clean root color, grey blending, or a richer all over tone when the goal is clarity, depth, and quiet polish.

Light

Face Frame Highlight

1 hour

Brightness placed around the face for lift, contour, and movement without committing to a full blonding service.

Dimension

Partial Highlight

1.5 hours

Strategic lightness through the top and sides for a natural, dimensional effect with a softer grow out.

Fullness

Full Highlight

3+ hours

Lightness placed throughout for maximum dimension, brightness, and a more complete color shift.

Blonde

Double Retouch

3.5 hr+

Regrowth lightening and toning for existing all over blondes or creative color clients maintaining a solid canvas.

Transformation

Double Transformation

By consultation only

A full head lightening and toning service for a major color shift, approached with condition and finish as the priority.

Add on

After Color Blowdry

+ 30 minutes

Can be added to any color service for a finished, polished dry down after your appointment.

Treatment

K18 Treatment

30 minutes stand alone. + 20 minutes with color.

A bond repair treatment for strength, softness, and a cleaner finish. It can be booked on its own or added to any color service.
