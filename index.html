Joseph Huckabee

Joseph is a Manhattan based colorist.

He is known for his expertise in creating both lived-in and transformative color.

He has completed advanced training in New York City and Berlin, training under world industy leaders.

His work has been featured on screen and in magazines, as well as on runways in Paris and New York.
