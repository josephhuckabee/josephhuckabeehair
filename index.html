Joseph is recognized for crafting haircolor that blend the skills of creating organic, lived-in lockes along with bold transformative changes. His focus on harmonizing natural beauty with modern aesthetics aims to create thoughtful haircolor that allows his clients to feel confident and empowered.

Joseph has had With advanced training in New York City and Berlin, training under industry leading artists. He is currently based in a top salon in the famous SoHo neighborhood of New York City. His work has been featured in magazines, on-screen, and at notable fashion runways in Paris and New York. Each project is a reflection of his journey to master color, striving to bring authenticity to his designs.

Drawing inspiration from art, nature, and the diverse energey of life, Joseph embraces new ideas to grow as an artist.
