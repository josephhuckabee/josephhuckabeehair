Joseph Huckabee is a Southern California born, New York City based color specialist with over a decade of experience focused exclusively on hair color.

His work is shaped by Los Angeles, New York, and European training, including time under celebrity colorists and industry leaders, experience in top NYC salons, and a six month apprenticeship in Berlin. That background gives his color a balance of editorial taste, technical precision, and wearable polish.

Joseph specializes across the full color spectrum, from expensive blondes and dimensional brunettes to unique vivids, glosses, and major transformations. His chair is built for clients in NYC who want thoughtful color, honest guidance, and an experience where every person feels welcome.
